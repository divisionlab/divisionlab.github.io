What are we ?

You've made up your mind to learn a new language so you can reach the dream of traveling around the world. You are all fired up and you download a language learning app like all the other ones in the market but after a few minutes, you never clicked on it twice. Doesn't this scenerio seem familiar?
Division Lab is an app that help people who doesn't have enough self-control when it comes to learning a new language. With this app, you'll be mastering a new language in no time

Why are we better ?

Decide

Decide your own vocabulary list. Learn what you want to learn.

Just Do it

You are forced to learn. Laziness is not an excuse anymore.

Division

An additional game that connects learning with entertainment. Let the germs divide!

Memorize vocabularys everytime you unlock

To enhance your impression to certain vocabularies, a vocabulary with its definition and sample sentence or a simple question will be shown every time when you unlock your mobile phone.

Customized vocabulary list

Ask you to tag some vocabularies that you are not familiar with. We will make it as a question or show their information to you every time when you unlock your mobile phone.

Different packages
to choose from

We’ve prepared numerous vocabularies with big range of topics and difficulties. You can choose from high school level to TOEIC, TOFEL level and different kind of vocabularies used in daily conversation.

Performance Analysis

We will record the number of vocabularies you tag/untag and the number of question you get right/wrong answers in. With all information, we’ll show you your own learning performance analysis.

Games to keep you involved

We design a simple game to make a connection with learning and entertainment. The background is set in a laboratory, your germ will grow with the number of learned vocabularies and lose energy when you don’t come to app for a long time. Through this game, you can grow with your pet germ simultaneously.
